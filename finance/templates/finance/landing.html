Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member?

Simply click the "Get Started" button, register an account, and make your first deposit via M-Pesa.

What is the difference between Savings and Shares?

Savings (Current Account) can be withdrawn anytime. Shares are long-term investments that earn you annual dividends but cannot be withdrawn directly.

How are loans approved?

Loans are subject to admin approval based on your savings history and credit score. Most loans are processed within 24 hours.
